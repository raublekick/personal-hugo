## Video Games
🔗 [Big Hops](https://www.bighops.xyz/)

I finished playing through _Big Hops_ on Switch 2. It's a solid 7/10 3D platformer. I liken it to the _Mario Odyssey_ equivalent of _Super Lucky's Tale_. For someone like me who has an insatiable thirst for more 3D platforming, this one was worth checking out. You play as Hop the frog, and use his jumping and tongue prowess to traverse levels and solve puzzles. Not super difficult but there are some surprising challenge areas.
